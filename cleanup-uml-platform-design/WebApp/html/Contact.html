Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

How quickly can I book a cleaning?

+
You can book a cleaner as soon as the next day, depending on availability. Same-day bookings are sometimes possible for urgent requests. Simply check our booking calendar for available slots.

What areas do you serve?

+
We currently serve London and surrounding areas within a 30-mile radius. Enter your postcode during booking to check if we cover your location.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

+
No, our cleaners come fully equipped with all necessary supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer us to use your own products (for allergies or preferences), just let us know.

What is your cancellation policy?

+
You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee. Check our full policy in your account.

Are your cleaners insured and vetted?

+
Yes, all our cleaning professionals undergo thorough background checks, are fully insured, and receive ongoing training. Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities.

How do I pay for services?

+
We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure online payment system. Payment is processed after your cleaning is completed to your satisfaction.
